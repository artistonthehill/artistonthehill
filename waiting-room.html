The Waiting Room

Original oil paintings for NHS staff and the key workers — those who hold everything together — available immediately, paid for gradually, owned completely.

16 original oils · browse the collection below

Why it exists

One of the most common things people say in front of my work is "I wish I could afford one of your paintings." If you're a real person — keeping society rolling — I want you to have my work too. I was a volunteer lifeboatman for 26 years, broke for much of it. I know what key workers look like up close. These paintings belong on their walls as much as anyone else's — and I didn't want money to be the thing that stopped that.

What it is

A deferred payment scheme, nothing more complicated than that. You choose a painting, set up a standing order for whatever you can manage each month, and it comes to live with you straight away.

The terms

Minimum £30 a month. No interest, ever. No fixed end date — you pay until it's paid off. There's a dashboard to keep tabs on where you are, and you can take payment holidays if life gets in the way. The painting goes home with you after the first payment, registered with a TOAT token as proof of ownership. When your payments are done the painting will probably be worth more than you paid — the definition of an investment — and the TOAT will acknowledge it's entirely yours. You've started your original art collection.

The paintings

All originals, oil on canvas board. Professionally framed in white with the TOAT ownership token fitted before it goes out the door. Every one is a place I was actually standing when I painted it.

"In 1945, Hans Wilsdorf — founder of Rolex — wrote to Allied POW officers whose watches had been confiscated. He offered to replace them with a simple note: pay whatever you can, whenever you can, after the war. No contract, no deadline, no penalty. Most did. Some couldn't. He never chased a single one."

The original done-on-trust business

The Waiting Room works the same way. There's no credit check, no legal document, no penalty clause. Just a standing order you set up yourself and a painting that comes home with you. Miserable people don't buy my art — and people who buy my art don't let me down.

Who can join the Waiting Room

Open to anyone working in one of these roles

NHS — all staff Paramedics Firefighters Police Teachers Social workers Care workers Unpaid carers Armed forces Coastguard & RNLI Civil servants Delivery drivers

"If you're not sure whether you qualify, just ask. I'd rather include someone than turn them away."

How it works

1

Choose your painting

Browse the gallery below. The collection is always changing. When you've found the one you want, drop me a message — WhatsApp, email, whatever's easiest.

2

Set up a standing order

Minimum £30 a month, whatever suits your budget. No credit checks, no paperwork — just a simple agreement between us and a bank transfer set up on your side. It's all done on trust — miserable people don't buy my art.

3

It comes home with you

I'll frame it in white and fit the TOAT ownership token. The painting ships to you as soon as you've made your first payment.

4

Pay at your own pace

No interest, no deadline, no penalty for paying slowly or quickly — it's already yours either way. More sizes coming soon.
