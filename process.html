How it works

From first message to finished painting

01
Get in touch

WhatsApp or email. Tell me what you have in mind — a location, a person, an occasion. A rough brief is enough to start.

02
Reference & discussion

Send photos, describe the mood, pick a scale. For portraits I may ask to visit or arrange a photography session. For landscapes I'll visit the location.

03
Painted in oils

I work fast — most commissions are completed in days, not months. You'll see progress photographs. The painting is done when it's right, not when the deadline says so.

04
Framed & delivered

Framed in a bespoke frame, certified with a TOAT provenance token, crated and delivered worldwide. 0% payment plans available over up to 24 months.
